Client to Server Responses:
pong					[Unique Microbit ID]
prime					[Unique Microbit ID]		[boolean isPrime] [int number]
sum						[Unique Microbit ID]		[int sum]

Server to Client Requests:
testPrime				[Unique Microbit ID]		[int number]
sum						[Unique Microbit ID]		[int num1]	[int num2]	[int num3]	...

FUNC RESPONSES
[Procedure name]		[Unique Microbit ID]		[Space delimited list of responses]

ERROR RESPONSE
err 					[error code] 				[error message]

This means "err" is a reserved keyword, DO NOT USE.
